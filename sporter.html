WHAT WE DO

Spoter connects both amature and professional sports men and women in Zimbabwe to potential Scouts, Agents, Sports Clubs, local and international Universities particularly in the fields of hockey, swimming, athletics, rugby and water polo that are interested in finding a team to play for or respectively finding a player for their clubs or university. All this is done in a secure and efficient way.

All the potential players, agents, teams and Universities have been vetted before-hand to ensure the best possible match. To find out more, become a memeber today and experience a whole new world of sport.
